Bullfrog Blitz

Bullfrog Blitz is a Top-down shooter, in which your goal is to reach the boss and defeat him!

You do so by getting past all the bosses minions and making sure you have enough ammunition left for the boss.

My Contributions:

The Boss

To make the boss stand out from its minions I decided the boss needed different shooting patterns. I reused the laser from the laser enemy, but made it fire 3 lasers so that it had a greater chance of hitting the player. Lastly, I wanted the boss to fire lots of bullets at once with a lot of range.


To randomize the bosses attacks I made it so that when the player leaves the bosses range, the boss would change attack. Also, I made it so that the boss has a much greater possibility to fire his laser than to fire his bullets, each time he changes attack for balancing purposes.

Laser minion

All the minions of the boss needed to be not too difficult to beat, but also not to easy. So, for the laser minion I decided that when the player came into the minions' field of view, the minion would first show where he is going to shoot, and after a few seconds fire his laser. As long as the player would stay in the minions' field of view, will the minion keep firing his laser.

Learn more about my code here


Melee minion

While programming the melee minion I decided that I did not want the minion to relentlessly keep attacking the player. So, when this minion gets the player in his field of view, the minion will go in attack mode and make a swing against the player. After his swing, will the minion back up for a few seconds. And when ready again will attack once more. But if the player decides to attack this minion with their own melee weapon, then will the minion keep attacking for as long it can.
