Alive By Sunlight

Alive by Sunlight is a game in which your goal is to kill all the survivors.

If all the survivors fix all the generators, you lose. So lock them up and attack them with your axe!

My Contributions:

Player pickup survivor

Whenever the player encounters a survivor, they can press the carry key to pick up the player. This survivor can now no longer fix generators. The player can carry this survivor to a cage and can put them in said cage. But the player cannot carry more than one survivor and can only put one survivor in one cage.

For this to work, I needed to get all the information I needed from the survivor when the player picks up said survivor. If the player picked up a survivor, the survivor would no longer be visible and would get a secret state. This state activates when the player puts the survivor in a cage, only then will the invisible survivor walk to the chosen cage. This way, whenever the survivor gets freed, it will look like the survivor just walked out of the cage.

Free locked up NPC

If a survivor gets locked up by the player, the other survivors will go and set their locked companion free. As soon as the state from the other survivors' switches, will they move towards the cage. When they get in contact with the cage, a timer will go of. And as long as a survivor remains in contact with the cage, will it open up after a while. But if the timer has already started counting and the survivors get scared off by the player, will the timer decrease again.

Survivor's brain

For the survivors to do all their decision making, the survivors needed to have a brain. To make this brain work and not be a complete mess in code, a state machine was needed. In this state machine are the following states:

- Seek & repair generator: The survivors will go look for a random generator and fix it when they arrive.

- Flee player: Whenever the player gets to close, the survivors will run away and try to avoid the player.

- Seek & free locked survivor: If a survivor was caught and put in a cage, whenever the survivor switches state, will they go to this cage and free the survivor.

- Seek gate: Whenever all the generators are fixed, the gate will unlock and all survivors will run towards it to escape.
