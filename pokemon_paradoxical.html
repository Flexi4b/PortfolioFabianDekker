Pokémon Paradoxical (W.I.P)


Pokémon Paradoxical is a new spin on the usual Pokémon games.

If you want to know more, why not give this game a shot? (It's a W.I.P)

So Far:

Bygone Village

Bygone Village is the starting town in which the player will start the game. To the right is Route 1, which is inaccessible at the moment. Going down lives a woman who has lived in seclusion for a long time.



Encounters

For the encounters on Route 1, are now only Spinarak & Phantump. I would like to add more encounters, like Murkorow and Weedle. But for generations higher than generation 5, I needed to add the following generation in myself. Furthermore, I want to look into a day and night cycle for different encounter tables.



What Next?

First off, I would like to look into making events and battling trainers.

After that, I would like to continue making the map up to around the second gym.

When that is done, am I going to ask people to play test my game, for if they find any bugs (not the type).

At last, will I release the demo to the public and see what they think about my game.

But of course, that does not mean that is the end of it all. I am going to work hard until the game is done.
