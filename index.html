About me

My name was already visible on this page, but in case you missed it, it's Fabian Dekker. I have always loved playing video games, especially the Pokémon franchise. So, I have tried making my own Pokémon games, but never finished building one. Over these past couple of years, I have made many games I'm proud of, as you can see in the above projects section.

Personally, I love making 2D pixelart games, I have even made my own sprites and tileset for the Global Game Jam 2023! But I also have a passion of making 3D games, I like how you can make your wildest ideas come true and nothing is too crazy. Although I can make games now, that doesn't mean I'm done learning about new ways to make or better those games. So, I'm always open for new perspectives and would love to get more experience in the game development industry.
